# Parmind Positioning (Draft)

## Core Idea
Parmind is an AI-native personal knowledge base that acts as a **context layer for your AI**. It stores, organizes, and connects everything about you—your work, life, and goals—so any assistant you use actually understands your world. The result is a true second-brain experience: grounded responses, personalized insights, and collaborative collective wisdom.

Key message: **"Make sure your AI understands you, your life, your goals."**

## Pillars
1. **Dynamic Organization**
   - Dynamic Areas evolve instead of static folders/tags.
   - Smart Area Hints suggest where new knowledge belongs.
   - Connections & relations keep context discoverable.

2. **Exploration & Engagement**
   - Organized structure makes exploration joyful.
   - Parmenides (AI assistant) chats over your Mind, surfaces insights, and connects dots you miss.

3. **Collective Wisdom**
   - Invite collaborators to co-build Minds.
   - Keeps context updated across teams or trusted circles.

4. **Context Layer for Any AI**
   - Parmind becomes the trusted source of truth for downstream AI agents/copilots.
   - Ensures assistants respond with your voice, priorities, and historical knowledge.

## Competitive Angle (High Level)
- **Notion AI / Notion** – Flexible, huge ecosystem, but clutter-prone. Parmind wins on opinionated organization + context fidelity.
- **Mem** – Great automated capture, limited structure. Parmind wins on intentional organization + collaboration.
- **Tana / Heptabase / Capacities** – Powerful graph/spatial tools with learning curves. Parmind wins on guided structure + conversational access.
- **Obsidian / Reflect / Logseq** – Local-first power tools lacking seamless collaboration and AI context layer.
- **Knowledge copilots (Glean, Guru)** – Enterprise search, not personal second brains. Parmind wins on personal + collaborative context creation.

## Positioning Statement (working)
"Parmind is the AI-native second brain that keeps your knowledge organized, contextualized, and ready for any assistant—so your AI understands you and acts like it."
